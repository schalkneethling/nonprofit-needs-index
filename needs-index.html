  • Golden Oldies Caring

    Golden Oldies Caring (NPO 220-963) (NPC 2018/619925/08) is a non-profit organisation how helps people (elderly, families and children) in need of food and cloths in Pretoria North supporting people with low income. The 3 volunteers are currently without wages.

    Volunteers from the community are helping with the packing of the food parcels and the sorting of all the donations and safekeeping of it.

    Registered with social development as a NPO but we do not receive a grant. The volunteers are South African, and they do not receive a government allowance. All fundraising activities have been affected, during the lock down. Any assistance is welcome but specifically we need help with:

    • Food Donations
    • Financial Donations (We must cover the packaging and buy the food needed for the beneficiaries that is not donated. )

  • Young World Educare

    Young World Educare (NPO 015-909) is a non-profit special needs respite care centre for special needs young children in Pretoria North supporting parents with low income. The 3 resident volunteers are currently without wages and basic needs.

    Volunteers from all over the world, sign up from February to November every year to assist with care giving and attempt to improve social competence and behavior, it provides opportunity for growth and a wider experience for our children with learning difficulty, mood disorders and disabilities.

    Registered with social development as a NPO but we do not receive a grant. The volunteers are not South African, and therefor do not qualify for a government allowance. All fundraising activities have been affected. During the lockdown we did not take in children and had given to parents payment holidays. Any assistance is welcome but specifically we need help with:

    • Food Donations
    • Financial Donations (We are unable to pay the full rent)

    Website

    http://crazyhatlife.info
